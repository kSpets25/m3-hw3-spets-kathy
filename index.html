About

Milton Glaser was born in (1929)in the Bronx of new York. He was born to immigrants who migrated here from Hungry. He attended The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Schience and Art studio on a full scholarship. He also studied in Bologna, Italy. Glaser co-founded Push Pin studios, a New York Company created by a group of graphic designers that created and expnded a type of collaborative graphic expression of visual art and unique styles. He started his own company called Milton Glaser, Inc. in 1974. Glaser was also part of the WBMG which is a design publication firm. Throughout his evoling career he contributed some of the most iconic icons, artwork and printwork that makes him one of the greatest, most well know, graphic designmers in United States history.
